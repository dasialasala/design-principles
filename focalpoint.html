Definition (by the book)

"...The center of attention in the design or layout" (53).

Definition (in my words)

The focal point is the element on the design that the viewer's eyes are drawn to first. There are two main rules of focal points. Rule one is to have one, just in general, and rule two is to have one per page.The focal points main purposed is to act as the visual interest of a layout.

Real World Connection

My eyes are drawn to a focal point everywhere I go. Even in passing, if I see someone with a really vibrant shirt, my eyes are instantly drawn to the person. A focal point is an element of emphasis that an individual's eyes are naturally drawn to. I often notice myself being drawn to a focal point when looking at food. I am drawn to foods with a lot of color that are visually appealing, making me have the desire to eat whatever I am looking at.
