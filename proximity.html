Definition (by the book)

"We perceive objects that are close together as belonging to the same group... we perceive objects moving in the same direction as part of the same group" (60).

Definition (in my words)

Proximity is relational closeness. The manner in which elements are positioned together as a group dictates their relationship. If items are spread out away from each other, they are likely not related and items that are closer together are likely to be connected in some way.

Real World Connection

Being that I am a pretty organized girl, I like to group my items in particular boxes and containers. The items belonging in the same containers are connected. All of my medical supplies is within the same proximity whereas my school supplies is tucked away in a separate place because the two are not connected.
