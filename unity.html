Definition (by the book)

"Unity means that all the parts of the design work together, and everything looks like it belongs together" (60).

Definition (in my words)

Unity is all elements of an image working together to create a final product. Nothing in the design appears to be out of place and there is an evident visual flow. There is a constant sense of consistency and visual satisfaction because not one element is overpowering another.

Real World Connection

I view unity when I see my neighbor's garden.Each flower that she has chosen and where she positions them gives her garden visual completeness. Everything is uniformed and there is no centerpiece or focal point. When I stare at it, I look at it as a whole, my eyes do not gravitate to one plant more than the others.
