Definition (by the book)

"A pattern, whether regular or irregular, also may create a sort of movement we would call rhythm.. Repeating pictures... Repeating a color... Grouping several phtos... Repeating your fonts...a visual sense of togetherness for the layout..." (59).

Definition (in my words)

Rhythm is a continuous flow of motion. Often, it is a repeated sequence and allows for an image to have a sense of structure as the eyes constantly have something to follow. Rhythm can be created with colors, lines, patterns, and sequences.

Real World Connection

When I think of rhythm in my day-to-day life, I immediately think of music. The strategic use of intruments and beats to create a melodic flow of sounds that are appealing to the ears. Rhythm is one of the principles that can stimulate all of the sense, not just sight.
