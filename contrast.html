Definition (by the book)

"You can employ contrast between filled and empty spaces. You may juxtapose contrasting sorts of lines or shapes. Introducing a pattern in proximity to no pattern results in contrast...Color and value also offer powerful contrast tools" 56.

Definition (in my words)

Contrast, to me, involves juxtaposing two different elements of an image. This involves working two opposing elements against each other. Often times, this involves working light and dark colors in order to create visual depth.

Real World Connection

An example of contrast that is very prevalent in my life occurs when I am picking my outfits for the day. I never want to be wearing an outfit where every article of clothing with the same color value. This is why I am often seen wearing light value colors for my tops and shoes and low contrasting colors for my bottoms. It is more visually appealing to me.
